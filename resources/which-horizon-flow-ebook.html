Decision matrix

Choose by the job you need done

Choose SEO After AI when...

  • Your website is not showing up clearly in AI answers or Google AI Overviews.
  • You need service pages, proof links, FAQ answers, schema, citations, and source-of-truth checks.
  • Your risk is AI tools inventing policies, prices, service areas, availability, credentials, or guarantees.
  • You want classic SEO basics plus answer-engine visibility without spammy AEO shortcuts.
  • Your bottleneck is discoverability, trust, and clear website evidence.

Choose Small Business AI Profit Kit when...

  • You need reusable prompts for admin, marketing, sales, service, or operations work.
  • You want to turn scattered AI experiments into repeatable workflows.
  • Your team needs human review rules before customer-facing AI output.
  • You want a 30-day rollout path instead of a folder of random prompts.
  • Your bottleneck is time, consistency, documentation, or task handoff.

Choose Local Lead Rescue System when...

  • Calls, forms, DMs, referrals, or old estimates are not getting a clear next step.
  • You buy ads or receive local inquiries but response speed is inconsistent.
  • You need follow-up scripts, text-back language, intake labels, and simple trackers.
  • Your team needs a clear owner for every lead.
  • Your bottleneck is revenue leakage from existing demand.

Use both when...

  • You run a local service business and also want broader AI-assisted operations.
  • You need scripts and trackers now, then prompts and SOPs once leads stop leaking.
  • You want AI to help draft replies, summaries, qualification notes, and internal process docs.
  • You have enough inbound activity that better follow-up and better systems both matter.

If/then rules

Fast rules for picking the right path

  1. If your biggest leak is unanswered inquiries, start with Local Lead Rescue System.
  2. If your biggest leak is staff time spent rewriting the same work, start with Small Business AI Profit Kit.
  3. If AI search tools quote the wrong facts, run the AI search audit before changing product pages or service pages.
  4. If you have no lead tracking at all, use the missed-lead checklist before choosing a paid resource.
  5. If you already respond quickly but work feels manual, use the AI checklist and rollout tracker before the AI Profit Kit.
  6. If you run ads, SEO, referrals, or marketplace listings, fix follow-up before spending more on traffic.
  7. If you have a team but no SOPs, use the AI Profit Kit to create reviewable workflows.
  8. If you are unsure, complete one free checklist from each category and buy the ebook tied to the bigger score gap.
  9. If you sell services online, SEO After AI and Local Lead Rescue work together: one improves discovery, the other protects the inquiry after it arrives.

Best default sequence

For most local service businesses: clarify public answers, stop missed leads, then use AI to improve repeatable operations.

  1. Run the AI search visibility audit if search/AI answers are unclear.
  2. Run the missed-lead audit and install a speed-to-lead reply rhythm.
  3. Use AI for summaries, drafts, SOPs, and follow-up consistency.

Combo recommendation

The strongest combination for local service businesses

If your business depends on booked calls, estimates, appointments, or jobs, the best combo is usually:

  1. SEO After AI first if the main problem is visibility, page clarity, or AI answers inventing facts.
  2. Local Lead Rescue System first if the main problem is inquiries going unanswered or stale after they arrive.
  3. Small Business AI Profit Kit next to turn the repeatable parts of lead handling, admin work, marketing, and operations into reviewed AI-assisted workflows.

This order protects near-term revenue before expanding into broader productivity gains.
