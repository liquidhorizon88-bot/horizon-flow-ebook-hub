Move-In Walkthrough Punch List Checklist for Property Managers

Use this worksheet before a leasing coordinator, resident-services inbox, maintenance scheduler, or AI assistant answers a new resident's move-in walkthrough, punch-list, damage, or first-week repair question. It keeps replies tied to verified photos, lease records, inspection notes, and vendor capacity instead of guessing from a resident message.

See Local Lead Rescue System

Use this when

  • A new resident reports move-in damage, missing items, cleanliness issues, appliance problems, key/fob/access gaps, or unfinished punch-list work.
  • Staff need to separate documented move-in condition, new maintenance requests, vendor scheduling, deposit-sensitive evidence, and resident-safe acknowledgement.
  • An AI workflow may summarize photos or messages and accidentally promise a repair date, rent credit, deposit outcome, charge waiver, or completed work.

STOP AUTOMATION if: photos conflict with records, habitability/safety is involved, deposit deductions are discussed, resident requests rent credit or fee waiver, repair timing is uncertain, access/key details are sensitive, or legal/lease language is needed.

Resident-safe snippets

Two acknowledgements that do not overpromise

1. Punch-list received

Hi [Name], thanks for sending this move-in note. We received the details for [issue]. We are comparing it with the move-in inspection record and will route the next step through [approved channel]. Please add any clear photos or notes in [portal/channel] so the record stays complete.

2. Manager/vendor review needed

Hi [Name], I’m sending this item for review before we promise a repair date or outcome. The team will check the move-in records, any open work orders, and vendor availability, then update you through [approved channel] by [review window].
Human-review rule: do not let AI or front-desk staff promise repairs, credits, deposit treatment, waivers, completion dates, safety conclusions, or access instructions until the source records and responsible reviewer are confirmed.

AI prompt

Prompt for an internal move-in issue summary

You are helping summarize a resident move-in walkthrough or punch-list issue for internal review. Use only the fields below. Do not invent damage cause, repair timing, deposit impact, rent credits, fee waivers, warranty coverage, vendor availability, access instructions, or completion status. Output: (1) issue summary, (2) source records checked, (3) missing proof, (4) safe resident acknowledgement, (5) CRM labels, (6) reviewer/vendor owner, (7) STOP AUTOMATION risks.
