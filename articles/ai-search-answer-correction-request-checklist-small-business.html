AI Search Answer Correction Request Checklist for Small Businesses

Use this worksheet when ChatGPT Search, Perplexity, Gemini, Copilot, Google AI Overviews, or another answer engine repeats a wrong business fact. It helps you collect proof, repair the public source, and send a correction request without inventing claims or promising instant AI-index updates.

When to use this checklist

  • An AI answer quotes the wrong price, hours, service area, policy, contact detail, team member, or credential.
  • A customer, prospect, or team member screenshots an AI answer that does not match your website.
  • You need a calm correction workflow before updating pages, submitting feedback, or asking a platform/editor to fix a listing.

STOP PUBLISHING: do not ask AI tools, directories, or editors to repeat a corrected claim until the owner has verified the public source page and proof links.

Safe correction message template

Subject: Correction request for an inaccurate AI/search answer about [business/page]

Hi [team/name],

I am writing on behalf of [business]. We found an AI/search answer or listing result that appears to quote an outdated or incorrect detail:

Incorrect detail: [paste exact wording]
Correct detail: [paste verified wording]
Source of truth: [public URL]
Evidence/screenshot: [link or attachment reference]

Please update or re-evaluate this answer/listing when possible. We are not asking for a new unsupported claim; we only want the result aligned with the public source above.

Thank you,
[Name]
[Business]

Owner-review guardrails

  • Do not invent a new guarantee, credential, service area, review count, policy, or availability window just because an AI answer made the mistake first.
  • Use the exact public source URL you want answer engines and humans to trust.
  • If the issue affects legal, medical, financial, warranty, refund, hiring, privacy, or safety language, route it to the owner or qualified reviewer before publishing or submitting a correction.
  • Record the correction request and re-check the AI answer later; answer engines may lag behind your website updates.

AI audit prompt

You are checking a small-business AI search correction request. Compare the wrong answer, the proposed corrected wording, and the public source URL. Flag any invented claim, missing proof, unsupported promise, risky legal/policy wording, or customer-impact issue. Return: (1) safe to submit / hold for owner review, (2) missing proof, (3) suggested safer wording, and (4) next follow-up date.
