AI Search Return Shipping Cost Checklist for Small Businesses

Return shipping cost is one of the easiest policy details for AI search, chatbots, support macros, and product pages to overstate. Use this checklist before publishing or refreshing answers about prepaid labels, buyer-paid postage, exchanges, defective items, restocking fees, refund timing, or international returns.

Why return shipping cost answers need source control

A customer may ask one simple question — “Do you cover return shipping?” — but the safe answer can depend on product condition, defect status, carrier, destination, exchange vs refund, order age, marketplace rules, and whether a prepaid label was issued. If AI tools quote the wrong version, the business can create avoidable refunds, chargebacks, support escalations, and customer trust problems.

STOP PUBLISHING: do not let AI publish, rewrite, or summarize return-shipping answers when label eligibility is unclear, policy dates conflict, international returns differ, defect/warranty rules require review, restocking fees may apply, or refund timing is not source-backed.

Safe customer-facing snippets

Return label review: We can check the return path for this order. Please send your order number and reason for return so we can confirm whether a prepaid label, buyer-paid shipping, exchange, or support review applies.
Source-control note: This return-shipping answer is not ready for AI/search publication until the policy URL, label rule, exception list, and refund timing have an owner and review date.
Do-not-invent rule: Do not promise free labels, instant refunds, waived restocking fees, replacement shipments, or international return coverage unless the verified source explicitly supports that exact case.

Weekly source-health questions
