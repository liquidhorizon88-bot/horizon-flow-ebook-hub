Use when

  • A job cannot start until utility lines are marked or a locate ticket is cleared.
  • A customer wants a start date before the locate/source record is verified.
  • Weather, site access, private utilities, expired markings, or conflicting maps make the schedule uncertain.
  • An AI draft is about to promise excavation timing, fees, arrival windows, or no-risk work.

STOP AUTOMATION: do not invent locate, safety, or start-date status

Do not let AI, autoresponders, or rushed office replies claim that a locate ticket is submitted, cleared, valid, marked, conflict-free, or safe unless the source record proves it. Escalate to an owner, manager, or qualified field lead before discussing dig safety, utility conflicts, property damage, legal responsibility, emergency work, fees, discounts, or guaranteed start windows.

Customer-safe update snippets

1) Locate request pending

Hi {first_name}, before we can confirm the start window for {project_type}, we need the utility-locate status verified for {service_address}. Our next check is {date/time}, and we will update you when the source record is confirmed.

2) Marking or source conflict

Hi {first_name}, the job file shows a utility-locate item that needs review before work can safely proceed. We are not going to guess from incomplete information. A team member will review {source_or_photo} and follow up by {date/time} with the next safe step.

3) Customer asks for same-day start

Thanks for checking in. We cannot promise same-day work until the locate/safety status and crew availability are verified in the job file. We will confirm the earliest safe option after {owner_or_dispatcher} completes the review.

4) Private utility or access hold

Hi {first_name}, the locate record does not cover every item needed for {work_area}. We still need {private_utility_or_access_detail} confirmed before scheduling. Please send the approved detail or reply QUESTIONS so the office can help route it.
