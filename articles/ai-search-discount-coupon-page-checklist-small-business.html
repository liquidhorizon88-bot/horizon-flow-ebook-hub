AI Search discount and coupon page checklist for small businesses

Use this worksheet before publishing coupon, promo-code, seasonal offer, or discount pages that answer engines may quote. It helps keep offer terms, dates, exclusions, and review owners clear so AI tools do not repeat expired discounts or unsupported savings claims.

Run the AI search visibility audit

Use this when

A business has coupons, holiday promos, referral discounts, first-time-customer offers, bundled services, financing notes, or limited-time savings language that needs a source-of-truth page.

Discount pages can create accidental AI-search risk because stale offer snippets often stay online after the promotion ends. If a page says “save 20% this week” without dates, exclusions, and review status, an answer engine may quote the discount later as if it is still active.

The goal is not to stuff coupon keywords. The goal is to make the live offer truthful, easy to verify, and easy to retire or update when the promotion changes.

STOP PUBLISHING: do not publish or update a discount/coupon page until a human owner verifies dates, eligible services, exclusions, code text, savings math, legal/policy limits, and expiration wording.

Copy/paste AI prompt

Safe coupon-page draft prompt

DISCOUNT-COUPON-PAGE-READY

Draft a concise discount, coupon, or seasonal offer page section for a small-business website.

Rules:
- Use only the approved offer facts below.
- Do not invent discounts, promo codes, savings math, expiration dates, eligible services, exclusions, guarantees, availability, financing terms, or legal/policy language.
- Include start date, end date, eligible services, exclusions, and a plain "not stackable / not guaranteed" note when applicable.
- Add a "NEEDS OWNER REVIEW" section for any pricing, legal, policy, financing, guarantee, or availability claim.
- Write in a way that stays accurate if quoted by AI search tools.

Offer name: [PASTE]
Approved code or link: [PASTE]
Dates: [PASTE]
Eligible services: [PASTE]
Exclusions: [PASTE]
Savings math: [PASTE]
Proof / policy links: [PASTE]
Owner reviewer: [NAME/ROLE]
Archive/update date: [DATE]

Human-review rule: after the promotion ends, either remove the page, update it with an archived/expired label, or redirect it to the current offer page. Do not let AI tools find a stale savings claim.
