Local service lead follow-up

The 15-minute missed lead recovery checklist

  1. List every place leads arrive: calls, forms, texts, DMs, referrals, and marketplace apps.
  2. Check whether each channel has a same-day response owner.
  3. Write one friendly reply for missed calls and one for stale web forms.
  4. Create a 3-touch follow-up rhythm: immediate, next business day, and one final helpful check-in.
  5. Tag leads by job type, urgency, location, budget fit, and next action.
  6. Review tone before sending; no fake urgency or misleading claims.
