AI Search Installation Instructions Checklist for Small Businesses

Use this worksheet before publishing or letting AI summarize installation, setup, assembly, mounting, first-use, compatibility, safety, or warranty-impact instructions for a small-business product or service.

Start with the free AI search visibility audit

Marker: AI-SEARCH-INSTALLATION-INSTRUCTIONS-CHECKLIST-READY

Use this when: a retailer, ecommerce brand, manufacturer, installer, home-service provider, agency, or local business publishes setup steps that AI search tools, chatbots, or support macros might quote as instructions.

STOP PUBLISHING: do not let AI invent installation steps, compatibility, measurements, tools, safety warnings, electrical/plumbing/structural requirements, warranty effects, code requirements, professional-installation needs, or troubleshooting advice. A responsible human must verify every instruction against an official manual, product spec, supplier document, qualified technician, or owner-approved source first.

Customer-safe copy blocks

Short page note: Installation details for [product/service] should be followed from [official source]. If your wall, wiring, surface, measurements, local code, warranty status, or safety conditions differ from the source, stop and contact [support/qualified installer] before proceeding.
Risky-answer handoff: I do not want to guess about [installation / compatibility / safety / warranty]. I am routing this to [review owner] and will answer only from the approved instruction source card.
