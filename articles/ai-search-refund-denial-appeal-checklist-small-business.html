AI Search Refund Denial and Appeal Checklist for Small Businesses

Refund denials and appeal answers are risky when policies, order history, payment processor status, exception authority, chargeback rules, and customer evidence live in separate systems. Use this source card before publishing any AI-search answer, help-center reply, support macro, or chatbot response about denied refunds or reconsideration requests.

STOP PUBLISHING guardrail

REFUND-DENIAL-APPEAL-CARD-READY: Do not publish, index, email, or let an AI assistant answer refund-denial or appeal questions unless a human can point to the exact order, policy version, denial reason, evidence received, exception owner, payment/chargeback status, next-review path, and approved customer wording. Mark missing facts as NEEDS OWNER REVIEW.

Refund denial and appeal source card

FieldSource to verifySafe wording note
Order / invoice / account ID[Order system, invoice, subscription, service ticket, CRM account]Never answer from a customer memory or screenshot alone.
Policy version in force[Refund policy URL, terms version, receipt copy, contract, program rules]Use the policy that applied at purchase or service date, not a newer page.
Denial reason[Outside window, used service, excluded product, missing proof, non-refundable fee, duplicate request]Explain only verified reasons. Avoid blame or legal conclusions.
Evidence reviewed[Photos, logs, shipping scans, usage records, support ticket, inspection notes]Name evidence categories without exposing private details.
Appeal / reconsideration path[Reviewer, form, deadline, allowed documents, one-time exception process]Do not promise the appeal will change the outcome.
Payment / chargeback status[Processor, chargeback portal, accounting notes, bank timeline]Route chargeback or reversal language to owner/accounting review.
Alternative option[Store credit, repair, replacement, exchange, partial credit, no alternative]Only mention options actually approved for this customer/request type.
Do-not-say list[Unsupported phrases AI must not repeat]Examples: “refund approved,” “appeal guaranteed,” “chargeback will fail,” “credit issued,” “policy exception granted.”

Customer-safe denial / appeal snippets

Denial with source

“Based on [policy/source] and [verified request fact], this request is not eligible for a refund under [policy section]. If you believe we missed evidence, send [allowed evidence] by [review path/date].”

Needs review

“We cannot confirm the refund outcome from the information available here. We are routing this to [owner/reviewer] to verify [missing source] before giving a final answer.”

Appeal received

“We received your reconsideration request and will review [evidence type] against [policy/source]. This review does not guarantee a refund, credit, or policy exception.”

Alternative offered

“A refund is not approved based on [source]. The approved alternative available for this request is [store credit/replacement/repair/no alternative], subject to [condition].”
