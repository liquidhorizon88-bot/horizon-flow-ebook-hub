Gumroad Audience Update Sequence for Ebook Creators

Use this copy/paste sequence only for people who already opted in to hear from you on Gumroad or your own list. The goal is to lead with a useful sample, show one implementation example, and make a clear paid-ebook offer without hype.

Open the free sample library Check the product page first

Before sending

Audience and safety checks

  • Send only to opted-in contacts: Gumroad followers, prior customers, or people who requested resource updates. Do not import scraped cold contacts.
  • Segment by interest: send AI workflow examples to AI-kit readers, lead-response examples to local-service readers, and AI-search examples to SEO/marketing readers.
  • Keep the promise modest: say what the checklist/template helps them do; do not claim guaranteed revenue, rankings, or automation accuracy.
  • Human-review rule: review links, prices, coupon dates, refund wording, and any customer-specific examples before sending.
