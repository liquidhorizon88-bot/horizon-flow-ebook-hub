Resident Rent Concession and Fee Waiver Request Checklist for Property Managers

Use this checklist before a property manager, leasing assistant, VA, or AI helper answers a resident asking for a rent concession, late-fee waiver, credit, hardship adjustment, payment-plan exception, or one-off policy exception.

See the Local Lead Rescue System

STOP AUTOMATION first

STOP AUTOMATION if the request involves legal notices, eviction/collection timing, fair-housing or accommodation language, hardship documentation, fee/legal interpretation, inconsistent ledger records, third-party assistance funds, payment-plan changes, manager-only concessions, or anything not already approved in policy. Route to the property manager, bookkeeper, or legal/compliance reviewer before replying.

Resident-safe acknowledgement snippets

  • Initial acknowledgement: “Thanks for the note. I’m checking the lease, ledger, and approved policy source before replying so we do not give you incomplete or incorrect balance information.”
  • Fee-waiver request: “I’m routing the waiver request for manager/accounting review before confirming whether any fee can be adjusted.”
  • Hardship or exception request: “I’m forwarding this through the proper review path. We’ll reply from the official channel with the next approved step.”
  • Third-party assistance or pending payment: “I’m checking the payment/assistance source and ledger status before giving an update so we do not quote a stale balance.”

AI review prompt

Review this resident rent concession, credit, or fee-waiver request.
Return:
1. request type,
2. ledger/source fields still missing,
3. whether manager, accounting, legal, or accommodation review is required,
4. a safe acknowledgement that does not invent concessions, waivers, credits, payment-plan terms, notice timing, legal outcomes, fair-housing conclusions, or balance details,
5. CRM label and next action.
Use NEEDS MANAGER REVIEW if the request mentions hardship, accommodation, legal notices, collections, assistance funds, inconsistent ledger records, repeated waivers, policy exceptions, or any unapproved dollar amount.

How this connects to Local Lead Rescue

Rent relief and fee-waiver requests are not ordinary inbox replies. A rushed AI-assisted response can promise credits, change balances, create fair-housing risk, or misstate the ledger.
