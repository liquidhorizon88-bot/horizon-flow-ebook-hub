Google Business Profile Q&A Checklist for Small Businesses

A practical worksheet for owners, agencies, local-service teams, consultants, and storefront businesses that answer questions on Google Business Profile, website FAQs, chat widgets, and AI-search-ready pages. Use it before public Q&A answers imply services, prices, service areas, policies, warranties, emergency response, or eligibility that the business has not approved.

When to use this checklist

  • A customer asks a public Google Business Profile question that could influence future buyers or AI summaries.
  • Staff, agencies, or AI tools draft Q&A answers for hours, services, pricing, appointment availability, warranties, financing, refunds, accessibility, or locations.
  • Your website FAQ, GBP Q&A, social replies, and chat answers need the same approved source of truth.
  • You want public answers that help buyers without creating unsupported promises or stale AI-search facts.

GBP Q&A source-of-truth card

Question asked: [exact customer/public question]
Canonical answer owner: [owner / manager / department]
Approved short answer: [1-3 sentence answer]
Approved source URL: [website FAQ, policy, service page, pricing page, contact page]
Applies to: [location / service / product / customer type]
Does NOT apply to: [exclusions, unsupported services, out-of-area cases]
Sensitive claims to avoid: [price, guarantee, emergency response, warranty, legal/medical/financial/tax advice]
If unknown, say: [call/text/email/contact path]
Escalation trigger: [when staff must ask manager]
Last verified: [date]

Copy/paste public answer templates

Service availability answer

Yes, [BUSINESS] offers [SERVICE] for [APPROVED AREA/AUDIENCE]. For the latest availability and fit, use [CANONICAL URL OR CONTACT PATH]. If your situation includes [EXCLUSION], please contact us before booking so a person can confirm the right next step.

Price or estimate answer

Pricing depends on [APPROVED FACTORS]. We do not quote a final price from a public Q&A alone. The safest next step is [FORM/CALL/TEXT/INSPECTION PATH] so we can review the details and give an accurate answer.

Policy answer

Our current policy is listed at [CANONICAL URL]. If a public answer, older post, or third-party listing conflicts with that page, use the policy page as the source of truth and contact [OWNER/TEAM] for review.

Prompt for drafting or auditing GBP Q&A answers

You are helping a small business draft a public Google Business Profile Q&A answer.
Use only the source-of-truth card below.
Return: (1) a short public answer, (2) missing facts, (3) risky claims to remove, (4) source URL to cite, and (5) STOP PUBLISHING items.
Do not invent prices, guarantees, refunds, legal/medical/financial advice, service areas, emergency availability, appointment openings, warranties, credentials, discounts, outcomes, or policy exceptions.
If a detail is missing, write NEEDS OWNER REVIEW instead of filling it in.

STOP PUBLISHING guardrails

  • STOP if the answer mentions price, eligibility, warranty, refund, emergency response, legal/medical/financial advice, or service-area fit without an approved source.
  • STOP if AI expands a short answer into a guarantee, exact arrival time, exact appointment slot, discount, result claim, or unsupported capability.
  • STOP if GBP Q&A, website FAQ, service page, and staff scripts disagree.
  • STOP if the answer is about a specific customer issue that should move to a private support channel.
