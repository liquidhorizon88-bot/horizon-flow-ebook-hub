Use this when

An AI receptionist captured the lead, but a person must own the next step.

Use this worksheet after an AI phone agent, answering service, call transcription tool, or outsourced receptionist creates a summary for a local-service business. The goal is not to automate the customer relationship. The goal is to make sure the human who replies has the facts, source notes, consent status, and stop rules in one place.

Good fit

  • HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, cleaning, landscaping, repair, and property-service teams.
  • Teams using AI call summaries, answering services, or after-hours intake tools.
  • Owners who need missed-call recovery without hallucinated promises.

Do not use it to

  • Invent availability, pricing, warranty coverage, diagnosis, payment status, or customer history.
  • Bypass SMS/email consent rules.
  • Let AI close, decline, or dispatch a job without owner review.

Human review rule

STOP AUTOMATION before replying if any field is unclear.

  • The customer mentions safety, damage, warranty, legal, refund, payment, or emergency details.
  • The AI summary conflicts with the transcript or call recording.
  • The AI receptionist guessed at availability, price, diagnosis, or prior customer history.
  • The contact channel consent is missing or ambiguous.
  • No owner is assigned for the next callback or scheduling step.

First replies

Use a safe placeholder while a human reviews the intake.

Callback confirmation

Thanks for calling. We received your request and a team member is reviewing the details now. We will call you back at [PHONE] by [TIME WINDOW]. If this is urgent or unsafe, please call [EMERGENCY/MAIN LINE] directly.

Missing detail request

Thanks — we have your request, but we need one quick detail before we can route it correctly: [QUESTION]. A person will review your answer before confirming next steps.

After-hours note

We received your after-hours message. A team member will review it [WHEN]. Please do not assume an appointment is confirmed until we reply with a confirmed time.

AI prompt

Summarize without inventing.

You are helping a local-service dispatcher review an AI receptionist transcript.
Use only the transcript and call notes below.
Return:
1. Customer request in the customer's words.
2. Known facts with source quotes.
3. Missing details to confirm.
4. Safety/emergency/warranty/payment/price/diagnosis flags.
5. Consent/channel status if explicitly shown.
6. Suggested CRM label and owner/due time.
Do not invent availability, diagnosis, warranty, price, payment status, customer history, or consent.
If evidence is missing, write NEEDS OWNER REVIEW.
Transcript/call notes:
[PASTE NOTES]
