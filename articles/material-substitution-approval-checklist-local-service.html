Material Substitution Approval Checklist for Local Service Businesses

Copy/paste substitution approval fields, customer-safe option texts, warranty/price/schedule review prompts, CRM labels, and STOP AUTOMATION guardrails before changing parts, materials, brands, colors, models, or repair methods on a local-service job.

Why this matters

Substituting a part, material, color, model, vendor, repair method, or brand can look like a small operations decision, but it often changes price, warranty, timing, code compliance, customer expectations, and technician instructions. Use this worksheet before a dispatcher, estimator, AI summary, or technician text turns a substitution into an unsupported promise.

Marker: MATERIAL-SUBSTITUTION-APPROVAL-READY. Use it only after the customer or owner has approved the substitution source, impact, and limits.

Customer option text

Hi {customer_name}, {original_item} is currently {reason_or_status}. We can use {substitute_option} instead. Before we proceed, please confirm you approve this change and understand: price impact {price_impact}, schedule impact {schedule_impact}, and warranty/fit note {warranty_or_fit_note}. We will not make the substitution until it is approved.

Owner review note

Substitution review needed: original {original_item}, substitute {substitute_option}, reason {reason}, source {source}, price impact {price_impact}, schedule impact {schedule_impact}, warranty/compatibility note {warranty_note}. Please approve, reject, or request more information before dispatch/customer update.

Technician handoff

Use only the approved substitute listed in the CRM. If the part/material on site differs, or if compatibility, finish, code, safety, or customer approval is uncertain, stop and route to owner/dispatcher review before installing or promising completion.

CRM labels

  • MATERIAL-SUBSTITUTION-APPROVAL-READY — approval, source, and impact fields are documented.
  • SUBSTITUTION-NEEDS-OWNER-REVIEW — price, warranty, compatibility, schedule, or professional judgment is not clear.
  • SUBSTITUTION-BLOCKED — do not proceed until customer/owner approves.
  • SUPPLIER-ETA-CHANGED — optional label for parts/material timing updates.

STOP AUTOMATION guardrails

  • Do not invent substitute availability, part numbers, colors, compatibility, warranty terms, fees, discounts, refunds, code compliance, or manufacturer approval.
  • Do not let an AI-generated summary approve a substitution on behalf of a customer, owner, licensed professional, HOA, tenant, inspector, or insurer.
  • Do not promise a same-day finish or no-cost change unless the source document explicitly supports it.
  • Route to owner/manager review whenever the substitution affects safety, warranty, scope, price, schedule, aesthetics, legal/compliance status, or property access.
