Customer complaint escalation log for small businesses

Use this copy/paste log before asking AI to draft complaint replies. It keeps confirmed facts, owner review, deadlines, public-review risk, and approved remedies visible so the team does not send polished but unsafe promises.

Use this when

A complaint may involve refunds, discounts, safety, legal risk, repeat service issues, public reviews, sensitive details, policy exceptions, or a promise that needs owner approval.

Complaint response is a high-risk AI workflow because tone can improve while factual control gets worse. A model can accidentally admit fault, offer a refund, promise a deadline, disclose private details, or summarize a customer history that is not in the record.

The safer pattern is not “AI writes the reply.” It is: log the complaint, confirm the evidence, assign an owner, decide what requires approval, then let AI draft only inside the approved boundaries.

STOP AUTOMATION: do not send an AI-drafted complaint response until a human owner has approved any remedy, deadline, legal/safety language, policy exception, public-review reply, or customer-specific history.

Copy/paste AI prompt

Safe complaint reply draft prompt

COMPLAINT-ESCALATION-READY

Draft a complaint response for a small business using only the information below.

Rules:
- Do not invent customer history, staff actions, timing, pricing, refunds, discounts, diagnoses, legal/safety statements, or policy exceptions.
- Separate confirmed facts from items that need owner review.
- Keep the tone calm, accountable, and specific without admitting unverified fault.
- If a public review reply is needed, write a short public version that protects privacy and moves the conversation to an approved private channel.
- Add a "NEEDS OWNER REVIEW" section for any remedy, deadline, refund, safety, legal, privacy, or policy decision.

Complaint source: [SOURCE]
Customer complaint: [PASTE]
Confirmed facts: [PASTE VERIFIED FACTS]
Unknowns / do not assume: [PASTE]
Risk flags: [PASTE]
Approved remedy options: [PASTE ONLY APPROVED OPTIONS]
Approved contact channel: [EMAIL/PHONE/FORM]
Owner/reviewer: [NAME/ROLE]
Response deadline: [TIME]

Human-review rule: the AI draft is a starting point only. A human owner must approve the final message and any remedy before it is sent.
