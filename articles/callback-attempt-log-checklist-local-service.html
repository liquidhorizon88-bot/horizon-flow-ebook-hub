Callback Attempt Log Checklist for Local Service Businesses

Missed leads are not just a speed problem; they become a memory problem when nobody can prove who called, what was said, whether texting is allowed, and when the next attempt is due. Use this copy/paste log to keep callback attempts honest and owner-reviewed before a lead is marked lost.

Use this before marking a lead as lost

  • Missed call: a prospect called but nobody reached them live.
  • Form or marketplace lead: the lead needs a callback, but the first reply did not get a response.
  • Shared inbox or front desk: more than one person may attempt a callback and overwrite history.
  • Human review rule: do not automate repeated calls/texts or mark a lead lost until consent, opt-out, frequency, and business policy are checked.

Copy/paste callback attempt log

Lead/customer: [name or lead ID]

Source: [phone / form / marketplace / referral / email / social]

Original request: [customer words only]

Consent/contact rule: [call OK / text OK / email OK / unknown / do-not-contact]

Attempt 1: [date/time, channel, owner, result, message left?]

Attempt 2: [date/time, channel, owner, result, message left?]

Attempt 3: [date/time, channel, owner, result, message left?]

Next due: [date/time + owner]

Stop reason: [booked / not a fit / no answer after policy cadence / wrong number / opted out / owner review]

STOP AUTOMATION: Do not invent contact attempts, consent, customer intent, urgency, availability, pricing, diagnosis, warranty, or opt-out status.

Three safe follow-up scripts

1) First missed-call callback text

Hi [Name] — this is [Business]. We saw your call about [service/request]. If texting is OK, reply with [one useful detail] and the best callback window. If not, we can call back at [real window].

Review: only text when consent and local rules/policy allow it.

2) Second attempt with one-detail request

Hi [Name], we tried to reach you about [service/request]. To route this correctly, could you send [one detail/photo/address area/access window]? If the issue is already handled, reply "all set" and we will close the request.

Review: ask for the minimum useful detail; do not request sensitive information unnecessarily.

3) Final closeout before lost status

Hi [Name], we have not been able to connect about [service/request]. We will pause follow-up for now. If you still need help, reply here or call [number] and mention [lead/reference].

Review: confirm cadence, opt-out, and policy before sending any final message.

AI prompt for a callback attempt summary

You are helping a local service business summarize callback attempts. Use only the notes provided. Return: (1) lead source, (2) request summary, (3) attempt history, (4) consent/contact rule, (5) next due owner and time, (6) safest next message, and (7) OWNER-REVIEW items. Do not invent contact attempts, consent, urgency, availability, pricing, diagnosis, warranty, opt-out status, or customer intent. If evidence is missing, write NEEDS OWNER REVIEW.
