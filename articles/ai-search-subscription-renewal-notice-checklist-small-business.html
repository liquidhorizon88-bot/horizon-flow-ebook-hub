AI Search Subscription Renewal Notice Checklist for Small Businesses

Use this worksheet before a website, chatbot, support macro, help center, renewal email, or AI-search answer tells a customer what will renew, what it costs, whether they can cancel, or what access changes after renewal.

Why this matters

Subscription and renewal pages often mix old pricing, grandfathered plans, cancellation windows, billing contacts, access changes, and special exceptions. If AI tools read a stale help article or support draft, they can invent a promise that finance or support cannot honor.

This card keeps renewal answers tied to named sources and human review before anything customer-facing is published.

Subscription-renewal source card

FieldCopy/paste valueReview rule
Plan/customer segment[Plan name, SKU, tier, legacy status, contract/account type]Do not merge legacy and current plans unless finance/support confirms the rule.
Renewal trigger[Auto-renew, invoice due, manual renewal, trial conversion, annual/monthly rollover]Quote only the trigger shown in the billing system or approved policy.
Effective date and notice date[Renewal date, notice sent date, cancellation deadline, timezone]Dates need a source link/screenshot and timezone before publishing.
Price / discount / tax / fee wording[Approved public wording or “send to billing review”]Never invent price changes, grandfathering, waivers, coupons, taxes, or fee exceptions.
Access after renewal or cancellation[Continues, pauses, downgrades, archive access, data/export path]Support/owner review before promising access, deletion, retention, export, or reactivation.
Cancellation / change path[Official URL, email, portal path, account owner needed]Use official channels only; do not route customers to an unverified form or person.
Escalation owner[Billing owner, support owner, approval threshold, CRM label]Any disputed charge, cancellation deadline, legal/compliance question, or high-value account requires owner review.

Customer-safe wording starter

Thanks for checking on your renewal. I’m going to verify the current renewal date, plan, and billing rule before giving a final answer. The approved source I’m checking is [source/link], and the next update will come from [owner/channel] by [time].
If you want to change or cancel before renewal, please use the official path here: [official URL/contact]. I won’t confirm price, access, discounts, taxes, or cancellation status until the billing record and policy source are reviewed.

STOP PUBLISHING when

  • The renewal date, cancellation deadline, timezone, price, fee, tax, or discount rule is missing or stale.
  • The draft mentions legal/compliance obligations, data retention/deletion, chargebacks, refunds, or collections without owner/legal/billing review.
  • The account may be legacy, custom-contract, sponsored, paused, on hold, or under a prior exception.
  • The AI answer uses “usually,” “probably,” or “standard plan” where the customer's billing record must be checked.
