Owner Distribution Hold and Reserve Change Checklist for Property Managers

Use this worksheet before a property manager, bookkeeper, virtual assistant, owner-update workflow, or AI receptionist answers an owner asking why a distribution was held, why reserves changed, when a payout will release, or whether an invoice, repair, chargeback, vacancy, deposit item, or resident balance affected cash available to distribute. It keeps the answer tied to the trust ledger, reserve policy, owner statement, invoice proof, bank/processor records, and accounting review instead of letting AI invent payout dates, available funds, legal/accounting conclusions, or resident-impact explanations.

See Local Lead Rescue System

Use this when

  • An owner asks why the distribution is lower, delayed, held, split, or offset by a reserve change.
  • Staff need to verify invoices, repairs, owner reserve rules, resident balances, chargebacks, pending deposits, or bank/processor timing before replying.
  • An AI summary might promise payout timing, explain accounting treatment, release funds, blame a resident, or waive a reserve requirement without approved source records.

Owner-safe snippets

Two replies that acknowledge without overpromising

1. Distribution hold under review

Hi [Owner Name], thanks for asking about the distribution for [property/period]. I am routing this to [reviewer/team] so we can compare the owner statement, reserve policy, invoice records, and trust-ledger timing before we state the final explanation or release timing. We will update you by [approved update window].

2. Reserve or invoice impact needs source review

Hi [Owner Name], I see the question about [reserve change / invoice offset / pending deposit]. Before we summarize the reason, we need to verify the source record and accounting review notes so the explanation is accurate and does not overstate funds available to distribute.
Human-review rule: do not let AI or front-desk staff promise payout dates, release held funds, explain trust-account treatment, blame a resident, or override reserve rules until the owner statement, trust ledger, source records, and approved reviewer are verified.

AI prompt

Prompt for an internal distribution-hold summary

You are helping summarize an owner distribution hold, reserve change, or owner-statement question for internal review. Use only the fields below. Do not invent payout timing, available funds, accounting treatment, tax/legal conclusions, reserve waivers, resident fault, invoice approval, or bank/processor outcomes. Output: (1) issue summary, (2) source records still needed, (3) owner-safe acknowledgement, (4) CRM label, (5) reviewer needed, (6) STOP AUTOMATION risks.
