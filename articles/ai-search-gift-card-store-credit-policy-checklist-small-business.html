AI Search Gift Card and Store Credit Policy Checklist for Small Businesses

Gift cards, vouchers, refund credits, loyalty credits, prepaid packages, and promotional balances are easy for AI search tools to summarize incorrectly. Use this worksheet before publishing policy pages, chatbot answers, FAQ snippets, or AI-generated customer replies about balances, expirations, fees, transferability, and exceptions.

Why this policy needs source proof

Small businesses often mention gift cards and credits in checkout pages, email receipts, POS systems, FAQ pages, old promotions, and customer-service macros. AI search can stitch those fragments together and invent a promise. A source card keeps public wording factual and gives owners a review path before customers rely on the answer.

STOP PUBLISHING: do not let AI invent gift-card expiration dates, fees, cash redemption rules, balance-transfer options, refund-credit exceptions, legal compliance claims, promo-credit eligibility, or replacement policies. Have the owner confirm the active policy and source links first.

Safe public wording templates

Gift card answer: Gift cards can be used for [eligible products/services] through [channel]. For balance or redemption questions, use [official balance/support path]. We review exceptions manually and do not confirm fee, expiration, or cash-redemption details without checking the active policy.
Store credit answer: Store credit is tied to [account/order/customer record] and may be subject to [approved limits]. If your situation is not covered by the policy page, contact [support path] so we can verify the source record before promising a credit, refund, transfer, or expiration change.
Internal AI note: STOP PUBLISHING. Do not invent expiration, fee, transfer, cash-out, replacement, refund, jurisdiction, or eligibility promises. Use only the active policy source card and route edge cases to [owner].
