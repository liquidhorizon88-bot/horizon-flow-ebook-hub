AI Search Answer Gap Log for Small Businesses

Use this worksheet to capture buyer questions your website does not answer yet, decide which page owns the answer, and avoid making unsupported claims just because an AI tool guessed.

Start with the free sample library

Who this is for

This is for owners, marketers, consultants, and agencies improving a small-business site for Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT Search, Perplexity, Gemini, Copilot, and classic search. It is especially useful when sales calls, contact forms, reviews, Search Console queries, or AI answer tests reveal questions your pages answer weakly or not at all.

Copy/paste answer-gap log

  1. Question or missing answer: write the exact buyer question, search query, sales-call objection, form question, or AI-answer weakness.
  2. Source of the signal: Search Console, sales call, support inbox, review, chat transcript, customer email, competitor page, manual AI-search test, or owner observation.
  3. Current page owner: homepage, service page, location page, FAQ, product page, pricing page, case study, policy page, blog post, or no page yet.
  4. Source-of-truth needed: approved service facts, pricing rules, eligibility limits, policy wording, proof URLs, staff credentials, licenses, photos, customer examples, or owner decision.
  5. Risk level: low clarity gap, medium conversion gap, high legal/medical/financial/safety/compliance risk, or owner-only decision.
  6. Answer format: short FAQ, paragraph, checklist, table, image caption, schema field, internal link, comparison note, or lead-form clarification.
  7. Internal links to add: pages that should point to the answer and anchor text that accurately describes the topic.
  8. Human owner: who approves the answer before publishing and who reviews it again when facts change.
  9. Publish decision: update existing page, create new FAQ/resource, merge into a broader page, leave unpublished until evidence is stronger, or reject as off-strategy.
  10. Review date: 14 days for quick copy fixes, 45 days for lead-quality signal checks, and 90 days before major positioning changes.

Human-review stop rules

  • Do not invent demand, rankings, traffic, revenue, or lead source from a single AI-search test.
  • Do not publish prices, guarantees, service-area promises, emergency wording, medical/legal/financial guidance, or eligibility rules unless the owner supplied them.
  • Do not copy competitor claims; translate only the buyer question into your own source-backed answer.
  • Do not paste private customer notes into AI tools. Summarize or anonymize sensitive details first.
  • Do not make every gap a new page. Merge overlapping answers when one stronger page would be clearer.
