Stop rule before AI answers

STOP AUTOMATION: do not let AI or autoresponders promise gate repair timing, emergency status, reimbursement, towing protection, private access codes, camera findings, police/security action, liability, rent credit, fee waivers, or accessibility accommodations unless the vendor ticket, access-control record, manager-approved policy, and resident-specific source record support that exact claim. Route safety, injury, theft, discrimination/accommodation, legal, insurance, repeated outage, or access-code privacy issues to a human owner.

1) First acknowledgement

Thanks for reporting the parking access issue. We are checking the current access-control and maintenance records before confirming the status or timing. Please continue using the official resident channel for updates so we do not share private access details in the wrong place.

2) Temporary access without overpromising

The approved temporary access instruction is: {approved_instruction}. If this does not work for your situation, reply with your unit, vehicle/permit details, and the time you need access so a team member can review the record.

3) Vendor ETA boundary

A vendor or maintenance request has been routed for review. We will share the next confirmed update from the work order; we cannot promise an exact repair time until the current source record is updated.

4) Towing, fee, or safety escalation

Questions about towing, fees, reimbursement, security, damage, accessibility, or safety need manager review against the current record and property policy. We will route this to the appropriate owner instead of guessing in this reply.
