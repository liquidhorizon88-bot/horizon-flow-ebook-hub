Use this when

Leads arrive from more places than one person can reliably remember to check.

This checklist helps an owner, CSR, dispatcher, or office manager turn scattered lead sources into a visible coverage map. It is especially useful after adding a website form, Google Business Profile messages, Facebook/Instagram DMs, Angi/Thumbtack-style marketplaces, live chat, referral partners, or an AI receptionist.

Good fit

  • HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, cleaning, landscaping, pest control, repair, and property-service teams.
  • Businesses where missed calls are handled but web forms, texts, or marketplace leads age out.
  • Owners testing AI summaries while keeping dispatch and customer promises human-reviewed.

Do not use it to

  • Invent lead volume, conversion rate, consent, appointment availability, or response-time performance.
  • Let an AI tool quote price, diagnose, dispatch, decline, or promise emergency response without review.
  • Scrape private personal data or bypass platform messaging rules.

Human review rule

STOP AUTOMATION before customer-facing promises.

  • The channel does not show SMS/email consent or platform reply rules.
  • No person owns the first response or backup response.
  • The lead mentions safety, water/electrical/gas, tenant access, warranty, price, refund, or damage.
  • The AI summary uses words that are not in the original lead note.
  • The reply would promise availability, diagnosis, cost, refund, warranty coverage, or dispatch timing.

Safe first replies

Use channel-specific placeholders until a human verifies the facts.

Web form

Thanks — we received your request from our website form. A team member is reviewing it now and will reply by [TIME WINDOW]. If this is urgent or unsafe, please call [PHONE] directly.

Marketplace lead

Thanks for reaching out through [PLATFORM]. We are reviewing the details before confirming next steps. Please send [MISSING DETAIL] here, and a person will verify it before scheduling.

Referral partner

Thanks for the referral. We have logged the request and assigned [OWNER] to review it by [TIME]. We will not confirm pricing, availability, or scope until we speak with the customer directly.
