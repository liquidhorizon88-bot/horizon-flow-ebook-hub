AI Search Search Console Query Triage Worksheet for Small Businesses

Search Console can show what buyers already ask Google before they reach your site. This worksheet helps a small business choose which pages to refresh for AI search visibility without inventing analytics, rankings, or demand.

Use this when

  • A service, FAQ, pricing, proof, or location page gets impressions but few clicks.
  • AI answers mention a stale service, old page title, or incomplete business fact.
  • You need a safe refresh queue before rewriting a site or buying more SEO work.
  • A consultant, chamber mentor, or owner wants a simple way to turn query data into action.

Stop rule

Do not invent Search Console data. If impressions, clicks, query wording, average position, or page URLs are unknown, mark them as unknown and pull the real report before making claims.

AI audit prompt

Prompt

You are helping a small business review Search Console query data for AI search visibility. Use only the verified data below. Do not invent traffic, rankings, query volume, buyer intent, business facts, services, prices, locations, guarantees, or competitor claims. For each page, identify: (1) likely intent, (2) what the page should answer, (3) missing source-of-truth facts, (4) risky or stale claims to verify, (5) internal links to add, (6) whether to refresh now, monitor, combine, or leave unchanged, and (7) the human owner who must approve the update. Data: [paste worksheet rows].

Human-review rules

  • Never paste private customer data, full lead records, or confidential revenue details into an AI tool.
  • Do not let AI guess why a query has impressions or clicks. Use it to organize possibilities, not invent cause.
  • Do not publish new pricing, guarantee, emergency-service, legal, medical, financial, or compliance claims without the approved source.
  • Keep a record of the old title, new title, update date, source data range, and next check date.
  • If the page affects paid ads, bookings, checkout, or legal policy, have the owner or responsible manager approve the change first.
