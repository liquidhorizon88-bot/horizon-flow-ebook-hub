AI Search Safety Data Sheet Source Checklist for Small Businesses

Use this worksheet before publishing, updating, or letting AI summarize chemical, cleaning, ingredient, storage, disposal, first-aid, PPE, or compatibility information that must come from an official Safety Data Sheet or owner-approved source.

Start with the free AI search visibility audit

Marker: AI-SEARCH-SDS-SOURCE-CHECKLIST-READY

Use this when: a retailer, maker, repair shop, cleaner, salon, workshop, contractor, property team, or ecommerce business publishes pages that mention chemicals, materials, ingredients, cleaners, coatings, adhesives, solvents, batteries, fuels, or maintenance products.

STOP PUBLISHING: do not let AI invent hazard classes, PPE, ventilation, storage temperature, incompatibilities, dilution ratios, disposal steps, first-aid instructions, emergency procedures, compliance status, or legal/regulatory claims. A responsible human must verify every safety-adjacent statement against the official SDS, label, vendor document, or qualified advisor first.

Customer-safe copy blocks

Short product/support note: For [product/material], follow the current label and official Safety Data Sheet. Our approved public guidance is: [approved wording]. If your use case involves heat, food contact, medical/skin exposure, confined spaces, disposal, mixing, children/pets, or workplace safety, contact [owner/support path] before relying on a general answer.
Risky-answer handoff: I do not want to guess about [PPE / ventilation / compatibility / disposal / first aid / legal requirement]. I am routing this to [review owner] and will answer only from the approved SDS/source card.
Page update checklist:
- Paste the latest SDS/source card.
- Remove any unsupported hazard, safe-use, disposal, or compatibility claim.
- Add a review date and source owner.
- Mark any missing proof as NEEDS HUMAN REVIEW, not as a published answer.
