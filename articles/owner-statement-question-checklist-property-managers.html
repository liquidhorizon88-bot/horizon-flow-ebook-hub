OWNER-STATEMENT-QUESTION-READY

Why this deserves a worksheet

Owner statement questions look simple, but a rushed or AI-assisted answer can invent a payment status, misstate a reserve, imply an invoice was approved, disclose a tenant detail incorrectly, or promise a distribution date before accounting has closed the period. The safe move is to acknowledge the question, collect the source fields, and route the final answer through accounting or the portfolio manager.

STOP AUTOMATION: do not let AI explain balances, promise distributions, interpret leases, approve reimbursements, change reserve amounts, state tax/accounting conclusions, blame a tenant or vendor, disclose private details, or close an owner question without verified source records and manager/accounting review.

Owner / property / portfolio: [name + property]
Question received through: [email / portal / call / text]
Exact owner question: [paste without adding assumptions]
Statement period / report name: [month + report]

Related rent/charge/invoice/deposit/reserve item: [source + amount]
Vendor/tenant/accounting/legal sensitivity: [yes/no + reason]
Distribution/reserve impact: [verified only / unknown / not applicable]
Prior promise or owner approval referenced? [source]
Safe acknowledgement sent: [yes/no + text]
Decision owner / accounting reviewer / due time: [name + time]
CRM label: [owner-statement-question / accounting-review / invoice-question / reserve-question / distribution-question / rent-roll-question]
STOP AUTOMATION until: [ledger/source proof + accounting/manager review]

Owner-safe acknowledgement snippets

Hi [Owner Name], thanks for flagging this. I am checking the statement line item against the ledger/source record before giving a specific answer. I will confirm the verified explanation through [channel] by [time/window].
Thanks for the question on [statement/month]. Because this may involve rent, reserve, invoice, distribution, or accounting records, I am routing it to [manager/accounting] for review before we state the final answer.
Internal handoff: Owner statement question for [property]. Exact question: [paste]. Statement period/source: [source]. Sensitive flags: [reserve/distribution/invoice/rent-roll/deposit/legal/accounting]. Do not explain or promise outcomes until source records are verified.

Fast QA before any owner reply goes out

  • Does the answer cite a verified ledger, statement, invoice, rent-roll, deposit, or reserve source instead of memory?
  • Does it avoid promising a distribution date, reimbursement, owner draw, reserve change, or invoice reversal without approval?
  • Does it avoid tenant/vendor blame, private tenant details, legal interpretation, or tax/accounting conclusions?
  • Is there a named manager/accounting reviewer and next-check time?
  • Is the reply going through an approved owner communication channel?
