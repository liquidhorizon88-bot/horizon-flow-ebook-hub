Stop rule before AI publishes or replies

STOP PUBLISHING: do not let AI, a chatbot, public FAQ, appointment reminder, or support macro invent deposit amounts, no-show fees, refund eligibility, credit transferability, grace periods, late-arrival rules, cancellation deadlines, prepaid package balances, fee waivers, or booking availability unless the approved booking-policy source and customer record support that exact claim. Route legal, accessibility, medical/safety, payment-dispute, chargeback, hardship, and policy-exception cases to a human owner.
