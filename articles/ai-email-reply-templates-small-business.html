Guardrails

The safe AI email reply rule

AI can draft the first version, but a human should approve anything that mentions price, timing, legal terms, refunds, account details, health, finance, warranties, or a promised outcome. Treat AI as a writing assistant, not a customer-service authority.

Prompt rule: if the answer depends on a fact you have not provided, the model should write [VERIFY] instead of guessing.

Email reply prompt card

Prompt card for: Small business email reply drafts
Owner: [ROLE_OR_PERSON]
Use when: [INBOX_OR_FORM_SOURCE] needs a reply draft
Inputs:
- Customer message: [PASTE_MESSAGE]
- Customer name: [CUSTOMER_NAME]
- Service/product involved: [SERVICE_OR_PRODUCT]
- Known facts we can safely say: [SAFE_FACTS]
- Private facts that must not appear: [PRIVATE_FACTS]
- Desired next step: [NEXT_STEP]
- Tone: helpful, brief, local, plain-English, non-pushy
Output:
Draft one email under 140 words.
Rules:
- If information is missing, write [VERIFY] instead of guessing.
- Do not promise price, availability, refunds, delivery dates, or outcomes unless listed in SAFE_FACTS.
- Include one clear next step.
- End with a human-review checklist.

Templates

Six copy/paste AI email reply templates

  1. Quote follow-up: Ask AI to draft a friendly check-in that restates the quote scope, asks if they want to move forward, and offers one simple next step.
  2. Appointment confirmation: Draft a short confirmation with date, time, address or meeting link, prep notes, and a reply path for changes.
  3. Support question: Summarize the issue, answer only known facts, flag anything uncertain as [VERIFY], and invite one next action.
  4. Referral request: Thank a happy customer and ask for a referral in a low-pressure way, without offering an incentive unless already approved.
  5. Stale lead nudge: Write a brief “still interested?” follow-up with a helpful option, not fake urgency.
  6. Review request: Draft a simple thank-you and review request, but do not ask only happy customers or pressure anyone for a positive rating.

Workflow

Turn one good email into a repeatable prompt

  1. Pick one recurring email type.
  2. Fill in the prompt card with real safe facts.
  3. Generate one draft.
  4. Score it with the AI Prompt Quality Scorecard.
  5. Save only the version that is accurate, brief, and easy for a human to approve.
