Use this when: a small business sells, installs, ships, rents, services, or supports products where wrong care instructions could cause returns, safety issues, warranty disputes, bad customer experience, or stale AI-search answers.

STOP PUBLISHING: do not let AI invent cleaning methods, chemical compatibility, temperatures, storage limits, installation steps, safety precautions, lifespan, certification, warranty coverage, warranty-voiding actions, or legal/regulatory claims. A product owner must verify every instruction against the official source first.

Customer-safe copy blocks

Care instructions for [Product]:
- Use only these approved source facts: [paste source card].
- Explain the normal care steps in plain language.
- Include a "when to contact us" note for damage, safety, warranty, or uncertainty.
- Do not invent cleaning methods, solvents, temperatures, certifications, durability claims, warranty coverage, installation steps, or safety advice.
- Add "NEEDS OWNER REVIEW" for any missing or risky instruction.
Short product-page note: To keep [product] in good condition, follow the care instructions listed here: [approved steps]. If you are unsure about cleaning, installation, damage, or warranty coverage, contact [support path] before trying a workaround.
Support reply: Thanks for asking. The approved care guidance we have on file is: [approved steps]. I do not want to guess about [chemical / heat / installation / warranty / safety issue], so I am routing that part to [owner/team] for review.
