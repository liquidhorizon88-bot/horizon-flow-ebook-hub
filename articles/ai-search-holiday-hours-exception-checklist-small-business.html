AI Search Holiday Hours Exception Checklist for Small Businesses

Use this worksheet before changing holiday hours, seasonal hours, emergency availability, appointment-only windows, or closure notices. It gives owners a source-backed way to update public pages, Google Business Profile notes, internal scripts, and AI-search answer checks without letting AI tools invent availability.

When to use this checklist

  • A holiday, weather event, staff shortage, seasonal service window, or local event changes normal business hours.
  • Google Business Profile, website footer, booking page, service pages, phone script, and chat widget may now disagree.
  • An AI search answer or chatbot has quoted old hours, invented emergency availability, or made an unsupported booking promise.

STOP PUBLISHING: do not publish or ask AI to draft hours, closures, emergency availability, appointment windows, or response-time promises unless the approved source record is current and an owner has reviewed the wording.

AI search audit prompt

You are auditing special-hours and availability wording for a small business. Use only the supplied source card, public URLs, and owner-approved wording. Flag any unsupported claims about business hours, holiday hours, emergency availability, appointment windows, response times, travel/service-area coverage, fees, or reopening dates. Return: (1) safe public wording, (2) pages/profiles to update, (3) AI-search answer risks, and (4) recheck tasks. Do not invent hours or availability.

Quick update checklist

  • Update the source page or footer first, then link other pages to that source.
  • Update Google Business Profile special hours and appointment links if the business uses them.
  • Check booking widgets, chatbots, autoresponders, marketplace profiles, and call scripts for old wording.
  • Add a rollback date so temporary hours do not stay live after the holiday or event.
  • Recheck one AI-search answer or chatbot answer after public pages update; record what source it quoted.
